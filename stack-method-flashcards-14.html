Opening

Hey (Seller's Name), this is Lesi, we've been discussing your property on (address). Do you have a few minutes to discuss?

Introduction

Perfect, we're very interested in this one. The owner was telling me he bought another property in this area back in 2024. He just had to step out of the office for a bit, but I'm sure he'll want to chat with you as well. I just wanted to go over our offer real quick & explain a bit on who we are.

About Us

We buy 3-5 properties a month, all for the most part via seller finance. We buy a few fix and flips every now & then as well if it's a good enough deal but primarily all is through seller finance. We operate the majority of these as mid-term rentals. Insurance housing, cooperate housing, co-living, etc. I'm not sure exactly what he intends to do with this one but more than likely it'll be one of those 3. We have many references so assuming you're interested I'm happy to send you some past sellers/agents we've worked with.

The Offer

We offer fairly competitive terms, full asking price, 30-40% down payment, and monthly payments on a 5-6 year timeframe. Have you had a chance to look over the terms we sent you? (Only say this if we have made an offer, if we haven't, tell the seller our offer terms).

Payment Terms

Payments are made on the first of every month, we set everything up through direct deposit to make things easy. We cover all taxes, insurance, maintenance. You just get a check every month and a final payment at the end of the finance period. That about sums everything up, any questions for me so far?
